What is halfCadence?

As a certified music tutor and former composition student,
many topics in music theory were beyond
confusing to me and my peers at first sight.

This website in combination with my youtube channel is an initiative to bring
the brilliance of music theory in an entertaining and informative way.

Whether you love music and want to know more about chords and harmony, or
if you have a test in four hours, this website has you set for a world of discovery.
